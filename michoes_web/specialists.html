Meet our specialists

Compassionate experts dedicated to your health. Each physician brings years of training and a personal commitment to Michoes.

orthopaedics

Michael Emusugut

MBChB, MMed (Ortho), Senior Consultant
28+ years joint replacement

Founder of Michoes, pioneer in trauma care in eastern Uganda. Trained at Makerere and University of Cape Town.

full profile
Special interests: complex fractures, paediatric orthopaedics, medical education. Past president Uganda Orthopaedic Association. Fluent in English and Ateso.
general surgery

Nicholas Iyamitai

MBChB, MMed (Surg), FCS (ECSA)
16 years trauma & acute care

Head of surgery at Michoes. Expertise in emergency laparotomies, soft tissue reconstruction, and surgical mentorship.

Additional training: Advanced Trauma Life Support (ATLS) instructor, laparoscopic surgery (India). Leads the hospital's quality improvement committee.
paediatrics

Adoa Pious

MBChB, MMed (Paed), DCH
14 years neonatal care

Director of maternal and child health. Established the neonatal unit and runs paediatric outreach clinics in Kumi and Soroti.

full profile
Fellowship in paediatric infectious diseases (RED-CENTRE). Leads the vaccination program and nutrition support for children.
obstetrics & gynaecology

Susan Atai

MBChB, MMed (OB/GYN)

Leads the maternity unit. Skilled in caesarean sections, obstetric fistula repair, and minimally invasive gynaecology.

full profile
Member of the Uganda Gynaecology Society. Trained in Kenya and Uganda. Advocate for maternal mental health.
