Michoes Medical Centre began with a simple idea: quality healthcare should feel like home. Today we are a trusted referral center in Eastern Uganda, blending expertise with a deep sense of humanity.

Our story

Founded by Dr. Michael Emusugut and a group of local nurses, Michoes started in a small dispensary. Over four decades, we grew into a 120-bed hospital with orthopaedic specialty, maternity, and a respected training center. The original thatched roof is now a garden where patients' families rest - a symbol of how we preserve warmth while advancing care.

We remain independent and community-owned, guided by a board that includes elders from Kumi, Soroti, and health professionals.

Values in action

  • Excellence - continuous training & modern protocols
  • Access - sliding scale fees & outreach
