Michoes Medical Centre began with a simple idea: quality healthcare should feel like home. Today we are a trusted referral center in Eastern Uganda, blending expertise with a deep sense of humanity.

50 beds

Our story

Founded with a vision to bring quality healthcare to Eastern Uganda, Michoes Medical Centre began as a small facility. Today, we operate a 50-bed hospital offering comprehensive medical and surgical care, with particular strength in orthopaedics, maternal health, and emergency services. Our growth reflects deep roots in the community and a commitment to accessible, compassionate care.

We remain independent and community-owned, guided by a board that includes local leaders and health professionals from Kanyum Kumi and the wider region. Every decision we make is guided by our mission to serve with dignity and excellence.

Values in action

  • Dignity - every patient treated as family
  • Excellence - continuous training & modern protocols
  • Access - sliding scale fees & outreach
  • Stewardship - of resources, environment & trust
