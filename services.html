Fracture care, joint replacement, sports injuries, and physio-integrated recovery.

Internal medicine

ENT, dental & eye

Medical wards & isolation rooms

ward features
- Oxygen concentrators at each bed - Negative pressure isolation - Palliative care team - Nutritional support
Outpatient clinics

Specialist consultation wing

Orthopaedic, medical, paediatric, ENT, dental, antenatal - open Mon-Sat.

clinic hours
- Mon-Fri 8am-7pm, Sat 9am-2pm - Outreach clinic in Soroti West every Thursday - Tele-consult available
imaging & lab

diagnostic menu
- X-ray / extremities, chest, spine - Obstetric ultrasound - Cardiac enzymes, HbA1c, cultures - 24h emergency technician
